<objective>
Build the flock configuration settings page accessible from the Coop tab. The farmer sets
breed, hatch date, flock size, supplemental lighting, tare weight, hen weight threshold,
and egg weight. These values drive the expected production model and egg estimation.

Purpose: Without flock config, the production model uses defaults that may not match the
farmer's flock. This is the calibration step that makes egg estimation and production
tracking accurate for the specific flock.

Output: FlockSettings component with tests and /coop/settings route.
</objective>
